Swachha Bharat

I and our batchmates went to nearby places from our residence. We had planned to demonstrate them how to keep our nearby area clean. Our aim was to make people nearby us aware about cleanliness and how to keep the surrounding and nearby area clean. We had planned an idea to explain them the importance of cleanliness, how they can keep their surrounding clean and what consequences can occur when it is not clean. After all this a explain them how keep the surrounding and environment clean. We performed our activity for spreading awareness for cleanliness. We taught them about energy conservation, clicked photos and successfully completed the activity. We conducted this activity for 5 consecutive days.

Food Packaging

I and our batchmates went to nearby places from our residence. We had planned to demonstrate the disposal of waste material in an efficient way to our neighbors. Our aim was to treat waste material according to their tendency and guide each and everyone to know the difference between dry waste and wet waste. We had planned an idea to explain them the after process of waste, how they handled, how they treated and what consequences can occur when it is not treated in a proper manner. After all this we give them the leaflets about waste management and show them how to dump waste in two other dustbins and distinguish between the dry waste and wet waste. We performed our activity of handling the waste in proper way. We taught them about waste management, clicked photos and successfully completed the activity. We conducted this activity for 5 consecutive days.

Plantation

I and our batchmates went to Open and barren land. We had planned to plant saplings in that barren land and spread awareness around the local people to fulfill our aim. Our aim was to plant saplings and make everyone aware about the importance of tree plantation and why is it essential to lay out saplings now. We had planned an idea to explain them the need to plant trees to better ourfuture live and to do our part as a citizen of India. After we successfully completed our idea about telling the local people about the importance of plantation. We performed our activity of planting saplings in front of them. We taught them how to plant the saplings and watered them. We laid the saplings on an open ground next to the mega mart, clicked photos and successfully completed the activity. We conducted this activity for 5 consecutive days.

Educating Government School's Children

We also asked the students there what their aim in life was, and accordingly we explained the needs and requirements of technology in each field, and also in the way of study. We performed our activity of educating govt school students about technology. We taught them about technology, clicked photos and successfully completed the activity. We conducted this activity for 5 consecutive days
